# Dental Clinic Website Plan

## Current Completed V1 Progress

- Built a publishable public website for a Muzaffarnagar dental clinic with Home, Services, About, Gallery, FAQ, Contact, and Book Appointment routes.
- Added a no-login booking request flow to reduce friction for new patients and older users.
- Added WhatsApp-first contact paths for appointment questions, service-specific concerns, and urgent pain/emergency intent.
- Added Hindi/English toggle, responsive navigation, desktop sticky actions, and mobile bottom actions for Call, WhatsApp, and Book.
- Added Google Maps embed, placeholder contact details, hours, credentials, reviews, gallery items, services, FAQs, and doctor/clinic copy.
- Reworked the hero into an immersive React Three Fiber scene using optimized GLB dental anatomy assets with interactive hover/drag highlights.
- Tuned the hero model scale, transparent scene layer, warmer enamel tooth material, and softer reveal timing for a calmer premium feel.

## Next Design Priorities

- Move the full-site background away from stark white toward a smoother eye-friendly clinical surface, likely warm ivory or very light teal-tinted off-white.
- Reduce visible aliasing in the hero by tuning renderer DPR, material contrast, lighting, and edge visibility without making the model heavy.
- Add restrained scroll effects: soft section reveals, subtle first-fold hero drift, service card lift, and gentle specialty-section depth.
- Improve conversion polish across the landing page: clearer booking reassurance, stronger local trust proof, warmer testimonials, and more deliberate service CTAs.
- Keep the visual direction premium and simple; avoid noisy cinematic transitions, excessive models, or effects that slow mobile devices.

## Later-Phase Backlog

- Real backend for appointment requests and clinic lead management.
- Admin dashboard for appointment metrics, source tracking, patient inquiries, and content updates.
- Optional patient login only if it clearly helps returning patients and does not reduce new visitor conversion.
- Automated reminders through WhatsApp/SMS/email after the clinic workflow is confirmed.
- Analytics for traffic, CTA clicks, WhatsApp starts, form completion, service interest, and device performance.
- Online payments only after the appointment funnel is strong and the clinic actually needs deposit or prepayment workflows.

## Product Principle

Do not add login, payments, or heavy admin features before the public website proves the basics: trust, clarity, speed, mobile comfort, and easy appointment conversion.
